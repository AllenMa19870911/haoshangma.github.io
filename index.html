About Me

I am Hao-Shang Ma. I was born in Taipei, Taiwan, R.O.C..

Hao-Shang Ma received the B.S. and M.S. degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Yuan Ze University at 2010 and 2013 respectively. He studied in the institute of Computer and Communication Engineering for PhD in National Cheng Kung Unversity and received the PhD degree in July 2022. Currently, he is an assiatant professor in Department of Computer Science and Information Engineering at National Taichung University of Science and Technology. Since January 2021, he is the Young Professionals Secretary of the Institution of Engineering and Technology (IET) - Taipei Network. His research interests include Artificial Intelligence, Data Mining, Social Network Analysis, Recommender Systems, and Nature Language Processing.

Research Area

社群網路分析（Social Network Analysis）

Due to the large amount of data generated due to the social interaction of a large number of users, the research on social network analysis can also be combined with the technology of cloud computing (Cloud Computing) to deal with it. In addition to pursuing the accuracy of social network analysis degree and efficiency, how to propose an algorithm that can be applied to huge amounts of data, such as various dynamically updated community analysis algorithms (Dynamic Social Network), community influence reverse sampling algorithm (Reverse Influence Sampling), or The use of cloud computing technology to improve computing efficiency in actual large-scale social network applications has also been the focus of development in recent years. On the other hand, thanks to the rapid development of Deep Learning and Natural Language Processing in recent years, social network analysis has also combined these technologies to develop. Network Embedding uses deep learning and neural network models To simulate the interpersonal relationship of users in the community, use low-dimensional representation to present user characteristics, replace the original community connection matrix, and have better performance for user grouping and calculation of user similarity. Sentiment Analysis applies language models to analyze the direction of public opinion and help governments or enterprises manage crises. When a major public crisis occurs in society, relevant discussions often spread quickly on social media platforms in a short period of time, affecting the public's trust in the government or enterprises. Social Recommendation also uses deep learning technology to learn the user's social behavior on the social network, the correlation between users and user preferences, so as to help the recommendation system to provide recommendations that are closer to the user's preferences. content. In the future, it will be one of the most mainstream trends to apply more deep learning technologies to simulate more complex social relationships and user behaviors to improve the efficiency of social network analysis in various fields on these issues.
